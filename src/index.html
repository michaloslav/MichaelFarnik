What I do
Front end
JavaScript - I've been working with in this language for almost 5 years. ES6+ JavaScript is as natural as English to me at this point.
React - My main specialty. I absolutely love this framework and have built huge projects with it. I also love its ecosystem, with things like Redux and Redux-Saga being a part of almost everything I do.
Vue - The community around it is still lacking which is why I prefer React but I think Vue will eventually prevail as the one framework to rule them all - the features are just too good.
Design - I'm a minimalist at heart and it shows in my work. I love designing simple, intuitive UIs and improving UX until it reaches perfection.
Back end
Node.js - I wholeheartedly support the JavaScript everywhere paradigm so it's only natural that I write most of my APIs with Node
Express - Express is really the most natural way to build APIs for me. It's usually my go-to choice for anything server-side
SQL & NoSQL - I usually use MongoDB but have worked with basically every major database type
System architecture - Since I'm a self-taught developer, I've had to learn how to both design and implement everything I want to make.

About me

My journey on the web started when I was 8 years old. That's when I learned the basics of HTML, CSS and programming... and then promptly forgot literally everything I had learned. But I still think those beginnings taught me to think like a programmer.

I really got serious about software at the age of 14. I downloaded some apps that taught me the basics of JavaScript, Python and other languages and just started playing around with them. I made games, websites, web servers, even some very basic machine learning and AI stuff. It was a whole new world to me at the time and I was fascinated by it. I felt completely lost in the sea of tech, tools and terms but slowly began to get the gist of it.

I gradually started to realize just how much I loved web development. It just felt like the most natural thing ever to me and I knew that this was what I wanted to do. So I started learning about frameworks, test tools, build tools and everything else, all while perfecting my knowledge of JavaScript even further. I found React and fell in love with it, an affection that still hasn't worn off. I also started reading about Vue which quickly became my favorite for smaller projects.

I finally feel like I understand the field now and it is the greatest feeling ever.

I have no clue where I will go from here or what I will build next. All I know is how thankful I am that I found something that truly fulfills me this early on in life.
